Our next story is titled: Tired of 5G? Qualcomm Says 6G Will Be Here Before the End of the World - Gizmodo. Qualcomm CEO Cristiano Amon told the assembled crowd at its Snapdragon Summit in Maui, Hawaii that the world can set its watch and clear its calendar for the next generation of wireless connectivity. 6G could allow for faster internet speeds, and the first “pre-commercial devices” with the standard will be here in three years. Consumer-ready devices with 6G will come closer to 2030, at least according to the chipmaker. Qualcomm has chips in a lot of different products, with the majority of them in Android phones.
