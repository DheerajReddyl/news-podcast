Our next story is titled: Nintendo’s new Fire Emblem mobile game has an Among Us-style twist - The Verge. Players choose to take on the role of either a disciple of light, aiming to find their way through the labyrinth. One of the three allies participating in each battle is secretly a treacherous disciple of shadow. After the initial battle, players vote to determine who they believe is the treacherous disciple. The outcome of the vote affects whether the next battle is more favorable or more challenging. Whether the disciples of light can correctly discover the traitor—or the disciple ofshadow can successfully deceive the others—holds the key to how thenext battle unfolds.
