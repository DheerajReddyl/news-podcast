Our next story is titled: Nate Bargatze Says He Didn’t Plan on Donating His Own Money for Failed Emmys Bit - Rolling Stone. Nate Bargatze says he never planned to donate $250,000 of his own cash to the Boys & Girls Clubs of America after the 2025 Emmys. The bit originated after CBS asked him to come up with a way to keep the acceptance speeches short. He said CBS was “very happy” with the idea, as was ‘everyone at home.’ He acknowledged that many of the critics didn’t feel the same way, but said he was trying to have a very giving night.
