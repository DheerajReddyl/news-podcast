The rate of cognitive disability in the U.S. rose from 5.3% in 2013 to 7.4% in 2023. The largest increase was among adults aged under 40. Adults with household incomes below $35,000 consistently reported the highest rates. rates among adults without a high school diploma rose from 11.1% to 14.3%. While most of the respondents were white, rates rose across nearly all racial and ethnic groups: American Indian and Alaska Native adults: highest prevalence. And that concludes this report.
