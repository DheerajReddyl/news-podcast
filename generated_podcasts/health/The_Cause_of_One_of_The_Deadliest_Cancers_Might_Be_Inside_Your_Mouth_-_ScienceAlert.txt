Our next story is titled: The Cause of One of The Deadliest Cancers Might Be Inside Your Mouth - ScienceAlert. Microbes living in our mouths linked to increased risk of pancreatic cancer. Pancreatic cancer strikes 1 in 56 men and about 1 in 60 women in the US at some point in their lives. Symptoms don't usually reveal themselves until the disease is at an advanced stage. understanding early risk factors, like mouth microbiome composition, could aid earlier detection, say the NYU researchers. The team aims to examine how viruses also contribute to these risks next. The study was published in the journal JAMA. And that concludes this report.
